Da Best Scrambled Eggs

Description

The Best Scrambled Eggs are more than just a dish—they are a morning ritual, a small indulgence that turns an ordinary day into something extraordinary..

Ingredients:

  1. ½ 2 large eggs
  2. 2 thin slices cooked ham, diced (Optional)
  3. 2 tablespoons sliced Cheddar cheese
  4. ½ teaspoon butter
  5. 1 teaspoon heavy whipping cream

Directions

Step 1
Gather all ingredients.
Step 2
Whisk eggs in a small bowl until smooth. Mix in ham, Cheddar cheese, and heavy cream.
Step 3
Melt butter in a skillet over medium heat. Pour in egg mixture; cook and stir until set but still moist, 3 to 5 minutes.
