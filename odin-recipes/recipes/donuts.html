Sugared Campfire Donuts

Description

Every bite of a Sugared Campfire Donut is a journey back to simpler times, where the warmth of the fire and the sweetness of the treat combined to create moments of pure joy. Whether enjoyed by the fire with friends or savored alone, these donuts are the perfect companion to a night under the open sky.

Ingredients:

  1. ½ cup white sugar
  2. ½ cup white sugar
  3. 1 (10 ounce) can refrigerated biscuit dough
  4. 3 tablespoons melted butter

Directions

Toss together the sugar and cinnamon in a bowl, set aside. Cut each biscuit into thirds, and roll into balls. Skewer on a toasting fork, and cook over hot coals, turning constantly, until golden brown. Dip into melted butter, then roll in cinnamon sugar.
